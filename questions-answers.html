How many students are there in a class?

In order to ensure the interaction and class quality, all courses are conducted in the form of small class (2-3 persons) live class, or one-to-one arrangement according to the demand.

How is the content of a course organized and how long does it take to complete?

At present, most advanced programming courses have 16 class hours each, 1.5 hours per class hour. If taken once a week, it takes 16 weeks to complete a course. For each course, we will learn the basic knowledge in the first 10 weeks, focus on completing an independent project in the last 6 weeks, and then take part in the competition with the completed project at the end of each semester.

When can I enter the competition?

At present, the purpose of learning programming is not only to learn programming itself, but also to assist students to make some achievements and prepare for future applications. Therefore, for each course, we will lead students to complete their own projects and then participate in the competition. Now we push every student to participate in the competition at least twice a year.

How to confirm the class time?

New classes will be opened every week for small class or one-to-one course. After the trial class, we will arrange a suitable new class for students. We will communicate with parents about the class time once a week or more times.

Our philosophy is to push children to exercise and try in competitions as early as possible, and to constantly learn and improve.
